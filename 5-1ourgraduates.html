Our Graduates

Name: Dewey

Birthday: June 10, 2012

Dewey is our organization’s first service puppy in training. This little guy is a Golden Retriever who was bred by CAMEO Golden Retrievers in Corapeake, North Carolina. He came to the organization in August 2012.

Dewey is a typical Golden puppy – he’s friendly, well-behaved, loves to play, is obsessed with water, and always seems to have a smile on his face. He and his puppy raiser, Megan Warhover, are practically celebrities on campus!
